What can I tell you about me?

WILLMADEIT is a Zimbabwean multifaceted creative and Zimbabwean Photographer. Best known for creative imagery and among the Creatives in Zimbabwe that have worked with reputable industry players and several professional and upcoming brands. Will Tanaka Chimboza Born in Harare, Zimbabwe in 1999, I have been crafting a niche for myself in the creative industry since the age of 19. Studying towards an Information and Technology degree I have been using different mediums from visual art, graphic design, photography, and cinematography

Wedding Photography

Wedding photography is one of the most challenging yet fulfilling genres of photography. It is a specialty that focuses on capturing people and events surrounding a wedding.

Studio Photography

Studio photography involves taking photographs indoors, in a highly controlled environment called a photographic studio. I will use a combination of prop, backdrops, lights, furniture and models to construct the perfect photograph

Portraits Photography

I aim to capture the identity, personality and essence of a subject utilizing photographic techniques such as composition, lighting and posing to give clients the best outcome.
